RockWash Geodata UK is a leading provider of the automated washing, preparation and photography of ditch cutting samples (rock chippings) from oil & gas wells. The application of the technology allows clients to digitally capture key lithological, stratigraphic, geochemical and hole-condition data. The service can be particularly suited to the generation of digitally accessible, national well database repositories having significant value with national regulators, new venture investors, current E&P operators & geoscience academia
