TSAVO OILFIELD SERVICES is a premier energy consultancy serving the Oil & Gas, Geothermal and Mining sectors within the Eastern African region. Its head office is in Nairobi, Kenya and it covers projects in Kenya, Uganda, Tanzania, Mozambique and Ethiopia. Its young team of engineers, supported by an experienced and talented group of consultants, bring a wealth of proven international and regional experience from projects with major global companies and independents in exploration, appraisal, development and production.
